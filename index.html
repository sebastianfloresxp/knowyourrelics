Classes

How the directory labels each relic record.

First-Class

Bodily relics such as bones, blood, hair, incorrupt bodies, and tomb-based remains.

Second-Class

Objects used by a saint, including chains, clothing, manuscripts, or devotional tools.

Associated Relic

Used here for major Passion or Christological objects outside the ordinary saint-based classes.

Collection Record

Used for shrines, museums, and treasuries holding many relics within one institution.

Class Not Specified

Used for imported inventory entries when the source confirms custody but does not classify the item by class.
